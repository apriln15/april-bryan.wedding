Entertainment

Discover the Wine Capital of Texas and the charm of this historic city while you are in town for our celebration. Centrally located between Dallas and Fort Worth, Grapevine, Texas offers local wineries and a historic downtown area.

You can also visit a variety of places during your stay here with us such as:

  • Grapevine Opry at the Palace Theater
  • Grapevine Vintage Railroad
  • Vetro Glassblowing Studio & Gallery
  • SEA LIFE Aquarium
  • LEGOLAND® Discovery Center
  • Lake Grapevine
  • Dallas World Aquarium
  • Perot Museum
  • The Texas Rangers Globe Life Park in Arlington
  • Six Flags Over Texas
  • AT&T Dallas Cowboys Stadium
  • Texas Motor Speedway
  • Lone Star Race Track
You can go shopping at Grapevine Mills Mall, The Galleria or Bass Pro Shop Outdoor World or check out any of the area golf courses. These attractions are all within close distance to our hotel recommendations in Grapevine, TX.
